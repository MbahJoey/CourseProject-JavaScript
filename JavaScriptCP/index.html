Cows and Bulls

There is a random 5-digits number. Try to guess the number. If the matching digits are in their right positions, they are bulls, if in different positions, they are cows. The cows will be shown as C and the bulls as B Example:
  • Secret number: 42716
  • Opponent's try: 12345
  • Answer: 1 bull and 2 cows. (The bull is "2", the cows are "4" and "1".)
